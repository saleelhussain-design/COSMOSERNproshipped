## 📊 3. Understanding Your Dashboard
The Dashboard is your "Command Center." At a glance, you can see:
* **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):** Your total sales, active tasks, and current stock levels.
* **Recent Activity:** A quick list of the latest updates in your business.
* **Quick Actions:** Buttons to perform common tasks like "Create Invoice" or "Add New Client" instantly.

## 🛠 4. Common Daily Tasks

### **Managing Clients**
To add a new customer, go to the **CRM** module and click **"New Client"**. You can track their contact details, purchase history, and outstanding balances.

### **Handling Sales & Invoices**
1. Go to the **Sales** module.
2. Click **"New Invoice"**.
3. Select your client, add your products, and click **"Submit"**.

### **Inventory & Stock**
Keep track of what you have in the warehouse via the **Stock** module. You will receive alerts when items are running low.
