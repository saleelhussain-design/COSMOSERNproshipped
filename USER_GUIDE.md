## 🛠 CHAPTER 3: DAILY BUSINESS OPERATIONS

### 3.1 Managing Your Clients (CRM)
*   **Adding a Client:** Go to `CRM` $\rightarrow$ `Clients` $\rightarrow$ `New`. Fill in the name and contact details.
*   **Client History:** Click on any client's name to see every invoice, payment, and communication you've had with them in one place.

### 3.2 Sales & Professional Invoicing
Creating an invoice takes less than 60 seconds:
1. Go to `Sales` $\rightarrow$ `Invoices` $\rightarrow$ `New`.
2. Select the **Customer**.
3. Add the **Items/Services** from the dropdown menu.
4. Click **Submit**.
5. Click **Print/PDF** to send the invoice to your client.

### 3.3 Inventory & Stock Control
Never run out of materials again:
*   **Stock View:** Go to `Stock` $\rightarrow$ `Item List`.
*   **Low Stock Alerts:** The system will highlight items in **Red** when they fall below your minimum required level.

### 3.4 Specialized Cosmos Features
*   **Visa Alerts:** Track employee visa expiry dates. The system will notify you automatically 30 days before a visa expires.
*   **HikVision Sync:** Your attendance logs are automatically synced from your devices to the ERP, eliminating manual entry.

## 🆘 CHAPTER 4: HELP & TROUBLESHOOTING

### "The App isn't opening"
**Solution:** Ensure your internet is connected and check if the "Engine" (the black terminal window) is still running. If not, double-click the Desktop icon again.

### "I can't find a specific record"
**Solution:** Use the **Global Search Bar** at the top of the screen. Type the name or invoice number, and it will find it instantly.

### "The system feels slow"
**Solution:** Restart the app. Double-click the icon to refresh the connection to the server.
